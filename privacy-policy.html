Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 20, 2026

1. Overview

Weather Keyword Highlighter ("the Extension") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains our data practices and how we handle information related to your use of the Extension.

2. Data Collection

We do not collect any user data. The Weather Keyword Highlighter Extension operates entirely locally on your device. We do not:

  • Collect personal information
  • Track browsing history
  • Monitor user activity
  • Send data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking tools
  • Share information with third parties

3. Local Data Storage

User preferences are stored only locally on your device using browser storage (chrome.storage.sync). This includes:

  • Custom keywords and categories
  • Highlight color preferences
  • Domain whitelist settings
  • Extension enable/disable state
Important: This data never leaves your device and is not transmitted to any external servers or services.

4. Permissions

The Extension requests the following permissions for functionality purposes only:

  • storage: To save your preferences locally
  • activeTab: To identify the current tab
  • scripting: To inject keyword highlighting on web pages
  • tabs: To manage highlighting across tabs
  • host permissions: To scan page content for keywords

5. Third-Party Services

The Extension does not use any third-party services, analytics platforms, or external APIs. All functionality is self-contained within the Extension itself.

6. Remote Code

The Extension does not use any remote code, external scripts, or dynamically loaded content. All code is bundled within the Extension package and does not contact external resources.

7. User Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can export your settings at any time
  • You can reset all settings to defaults
  • You can uninstall the Extension at any time
  • All local data will be deleted upon uninstallation

8. Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device and no data is transmitted, security risks are minimal. Your data is protected by your device's native storage security mechanisms.

9. Children's Privacy

The Extension is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children. Parents or guardians concerned about their child's use should uninstall the Extension.

10. Policy Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally to reflect changes to the Extension or legal requirements. Continued use of the Extension constitutes acceptance of any changes. Material changes will be reflected with an updated "Last Updated" date.

11. Contact & Support

For questions, concerns, or feedback regarding this Privacy Policy or the Extension, please contact us through the Chrome Web Store extension page.

12. Compliance

The Weather Keyword Highlighter Extension complies with:

  • Chrome Web Store Developer Program Policies
  • GDPR (General Data Protection Regulation)
  • CCPA (California Consumer Privacy Act)
  • Other applicable privacy regulations
